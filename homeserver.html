Security Advisory: FileCloud Security Notice

We would like to inform you about the security measures and guidelines regarding the files stored in FileCloud. While FileCloud provides a secure environment for file storage and sharing, it is important to be aware of certain considerations to ensure the safety of your account and files.

  1. File Security: The files stored in FileCloud are subject to your own responsibility and oversight. It is crucial to exercise caution and ensure that only authorized and legal files are uploaded to your account. Uploading non-authorized or illegal files may result in the deletion of your account and files.
  2. Unauthorized or Illegal Files: FileCloud does not support the storage or sharing of unauthorized or illegal files. It is your responsibility to ensure that the files you upload comply with all applicable laws and regulations. Any violation of these guidelines may lead to the removal of your account and files.
  3. Security Measures: FileCloud implements various security measures to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and regular security updates. However, it is important to note that no system is completely immune to security risks. Therefore, it is advisable to follow best practices for file security, such as using strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, and regularly updating your software.
  4. File Type Restrictions: FileCloud may have restrictions on certain file types for security reasons. These restrictions are in place to prevent potential security vulnerabilities and protect your account and files from malicious activities. If you encounter an error message stating that a file type is not permitted for security reasons, it is recommended to adhere to the restrictions and refrain from uploading such files.

Please remember that maintaining the security and integrity of your files is a shared responsibility. By following these guidelines and being mindful of the content you upload, you can help ensure a safe and secure environment for your files in FileCloud.

If you have any further questions or concerns regarding file security in FileCloud, please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team for assistance.

Stay vigilant and keep your files secure!
